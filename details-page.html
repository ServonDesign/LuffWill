A substantial five bedroom detached family home that has been extended remodelled rerfubished to a high standard situated in a popular culdesac within the highly regarded area of Camberley.

To the ground floor there is a large entrance hall with oak flooring & stairs to the first floor. The cloakroom has been re-fitted with a modern white Villeroy & Boch suite with tiled flooring. The 17' living room has patio doors onto the rear garden & oak sliding double tion hob with a glass splash-back, a stainless steel extractor, a stainless steel double oven, microwave and warming drawer, a fitted dishwasher, large patio & French doors onto the rear garden. There is a study with oak flooring that could also be used as a fifth bedroom & the 14' utility room has oak-fronted units, tiled flooring access onto the rear garden & into the garage.

To the first floor there is a partially galleried landing. The dual aspect master bedroom has a walk-in dressing room & a re-fitted Villeroy & Boch fully tiled modern en-suite shower room. There are three further double bedrooms & a fully tiled family bathroom again re-fitted with a modern four piece Villeroy & Boch suite.

Outside there is ample driveway parking for several vehicles leading to the attached double width garage that has an electric door. There are lawn areas mature trees & access to both sides of the property. The rear garden offers a high degree of seclusion with a full width shaped paved patios & a lawn area beyond that extends to the side of the property enclosed by fencing, mature bushes & trees.
